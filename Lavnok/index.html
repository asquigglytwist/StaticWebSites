About Lavnok

Lavnok's name is derived from Falcon, a bird that is much admired for its far-sight. It is this far sight that forms the core of Lavnok's design principle - A better design for tomorrow, makes a better world today. More than a principle, we're guided by this belief. And thus led, all our designs are always usable, accessible and hand-crafted to perfection. Our expertise ranges from Web to Desktop and Mobiles to Print but the one thing that defines us is our common interest - a passion for creation.

The Motto

We don't just create a product, we create a presence for you - one that speaks for itself both as a business as well as a promise. Be it designing a website or developing an app, we commit to it completely so that you can do what you love and let us take care of the rest. Every project completed is a business delivered but more importantly a proud accomplishment. We love what we do and take pride in sculpting everything to a pixel perfection. Our target never stops at completion and always lies beyond perfection.
We strongly believe that our success lies deeply in the success of our customers. Hence we say promise, Lots of sky.. Come, let's fly..

Philosophy

The primary guideline that we base all our designs with is to offer the best User Experience - in terms of Usability, Accessibility and Maintainability. Our designs are both responsive as well as responsible.
  • Responsible design includes
    • Always following Accessibility guideLines;
    • Per device Optimization - both faster and memory efficient adjusting to the device capabilities.
  • Responsive designs include catering to a wide range of devices - smallest smartphones to the widest desktop screens.
As individuals, we learn a lot from the society but as responsible designers, we also contribute back to it via OpenSource. To know more, please visit our GiveAways section or our TechBlog.
